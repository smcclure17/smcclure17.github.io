Welcome

This site is where I will share my work and sometimes thoughts, mostly in the form of project markdowns. The goal of this blog is twofold,

  1. To document, discuss, and share projects I've been working on, but more importantly,
  2. to force myself to actually do stuff.

Often I find myself with good ideas for projects, but without the motivation to see them through. After making marginal progress I abondone these projects for new ventures, leaving them for dead. I hope that having a place to display such work will incentivize me to see projects through.

Lastly, I built this site from scratch, (well with the help of some Bootstrap) so all of it's code, and code from future projects can be found on my Github.
